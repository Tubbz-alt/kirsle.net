Kirsle.net Solar

This is the first iteration of the Kirsle.net Solar theme. It uses lots of flat, static images rather than true transparency to support Internet Explorer 6.

This design went into effect somewhere around September 28, 2009, which is also when I moved my personal site from Cuvou.com over to Kirsle.net. It replaced the previous Cosmos theme.

I later gave it an HTML5 refresh and broke compatibility with MSIE 6.
